What are minimum requirements to install this App
Minimum android version for android app is v4.4(Kitkat) to latest
Player control Features
You can use swipe up and down to increase Brightness and Volume. you can swipe left to right to fast forward.
Video Quality, Speed and Screen Resolution
Video Quality and speed is depends on your internet speed or else you can choose quality settings from the player. You can change screen Resolution from player "FIT"
Does this app support Android TV/ Firestick
Yes, This same app supports Android TV, firesticks and Android box
